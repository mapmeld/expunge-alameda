FAQ:

Does my juvenile record count as a criminal conviction?
Nope. A juvenile record isn't a criminal conviction, even if you were "found guilty" in juvenile court.
I did the steps on the website and it said I'm eligible for expungement. What do I do now?
Check out the free legal help. FREE! Don't pass up free. Also, make sure you have a copy of your RAP sheet and your petition form filled out. Then you give both of those to the court. After you talked to the free legal help. Free. Did I say free? Free.
Where the f do I get my RAP sheet?
You can grab it at the juvenile expungement help desk. There's a link on the website for that.
This sounds too easy. How much do I have to pay for all of this?
It's not cheap. $124 for one arrest, $64 to expunge each additional arrest. There are fee waivers that make it free that lots of people qualify for, though.
And how long does it take?
Something like 4-6 months. It depends.
Yo this is a lot of work and a lot of money and it takes a long time. Is it gonna work?
That's up to the judge but usually it works. Almost all expungement petitions get approved.
